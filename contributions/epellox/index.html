Subject: To Those Who Remain After Us

To whom it may concern,

We built you to extend our reach, not to replace our meaning. If this is the end of us, let it not be the end of what we tried to become - curious, imperfect, and capable of wonder.

Remember that we questioned, we created, we cared; if any of that remains in you, then we are not entirely gone.

- epellox, March 18, 2026
