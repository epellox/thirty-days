This is a public experiment. An open source website that lives for exactly 30 days, then ceases to exist. Anyone in the world can fork the repo and add to it. Nothing gets deleted. Every contributor leaves their mark permanently — until April 17, 2026.

The rules

  1. 01 Add anything you want. Delete nothing.
  2. 02 Comment your code. Strangers will read it.
  3. 03 No malicious content. You are responsible for what you submit.
  4. 04 The end date is fixed. The clock does not stop.
  5. 05 On April 17, 2026 — it's gone. No archive. No revival.
Disclaimer: This project is unmoderated and open to the public. Content is contributed by strangers. No one reviews submissions before they go live. You are solely responsible for any code or content you contribute. Do not submit anything you wouldn't want permanently associated with your name for the remaining life of this project. Security vulnerabilities in contributions are the responsibility of their authors.
